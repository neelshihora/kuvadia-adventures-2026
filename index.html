Day 2 — Shenandoah + Luray Caverns
Sunday, June 28  ·  Three parallel tracks all day

group A · avid hikers 🧗🏻‍
Old Rag Mountain Loop👣🔗 -- 9.3 mi loop, 2,608 ft elevation gain, ~7–8 hrs estimated time. One of the most celebrated hikes on the East Coast. Full-day rock scramble with 360° summit views. Enter by 8 AM. Requires a timed-entry permit and standard park entrance pass.
Google Maps Link -- Old Rag Parking (Ranger Station)📍🔗
📝 -- This is a physically demanding strenuous hike. You will be using both the upper body and lower body strength to traverse the rock scramble portion of the hike. Please bring appropriate hiking-wear. Make sure to carry some food, at least 2-litres of water, and sun protection. Pack as lightly as possible for this hike.
group B · moderate hikers + older kids 🥾
Lower White Oak Falls via White Oak Canyon Trail👣🔗 -- 2.9 mi out & back, 406 ft elevation gain, ~1.5 hrs estimated time. Cascading waterfalls along a shaded creek. Take it slow. Great for kids.
Google Maps Link -- Lower Whiteoak Canyon Parking Lot📍🔗
Afternoon -- scenic Skyline Drive cruise with multiple overlook stops.
group C · seniors, babies, young kids
Luray Caverns -- largest cavern on the East Coast. Fully paved and accessible for strollers and mobility aids. Naturally 58°F inside — a summer blessing. The stalactite organ that plays music is unforgettable for kids. ~1.5 hrs. After they are done exploring the caverns, they can explore downtown Luray, ice cream, shops.
Google Maps Link -- Luray Caverns Parking Lot📍🔗
📝 -- Please look at the suggested plan listed under Day 2 section of Plan Your Visit to come up with a better plan for Group C
evening · all
Group cookout at the cabin. Everyone swapping stories. This is the heart 💖 of the trip.

Hensley Hollow Lodge — Elkton, VA
7 bedrooms · 4 bathrooms · sleeps up to 22 · 5 private acres · ★ 4.9
A 4,000+ sq ft log home with panoramic Blue Ridge Mountain views. Main-level king suites with private decks, plus a loft with queen and twin beds — ideal for kids. 8-person hot tub, fire pit, gas grill, Blackstone griddle, game room, full kitchen. Multiple guests report it handles large groups without anyone feeling crowded. About 20–25 min drive to Luray and all trailheads. Listed on TripForth, VRBO (#46072), and Airbnb.
