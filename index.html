group A · avid hikers 🧗🏻‍
Old Rag Mountain Loop👣🔗 -- 9.3 mi loop, 2,608 ft elevation gain, ~7–8 hrs estimated time. One of the most celebrated hikes on the East Coast. Full-day rock scramble with 360° summit views. Enter by 8 AM. Requires a timed-entry permit and standard park entrance pass.
group B · moderate hikers + older kids 🥾
Lower White Oak Falls via White Oak Canyon Trail👣🔗 -- 2.9 mi out & back, 406 ft elevation gain, ~1.5 hrs estimated time. Cascading waterfalls along a shaded creek. Take it slow. Great for kids.
Afternoon: scenic Skyline Drive cruise with multiple overlook stops.
group C · seniors, babies, young kids
Luray Caverns -- largest cavern on the East Coast. Fully paved and accessible for strollers and mobility aids. Naturally 58°F inside — a summer blessing. The stalactite organ that plays music is unforgettable for kids. ~1.5 hrs. After they are done exploring the caverns, they can explore downtown Luray, ice cream, shops.
evening · all
Group cookout at the cabin. Everyone swapping stories. This is the heart 💖 of the trip.

Shenandoah River Tubing -- ~2–3 hrs guided float on the calm South Fork Shenandoah via Shenandoah River Outfitters (shenandoahriver.com). Suitable for non-hikers, most seniors, and kids 6+. Book ahead for a large group.
late afternoon · all
Skyline Drive Sunset Cruise -- everyone in cars for golden hour. Stony Man, Thorofare Mountain, and Crescent Rock overlooks. Among the most beautiful views in the eastern US -- zero hiking required, just park and walk to the railing.
evening · all
Group cookout at the cabin OR Group dinner out in Luray (Book ahead for the party of 20+). Everyone swapping stories. This is the heart 💖 of the trip.
